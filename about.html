About the Mechanics

The Mechanics that work at Silver Star Auto Express are both experienced mechanics that have years under their belt and have been servicing vehicles for most of their lives. Born and raised in Guyana they moved and ventured to America, the land of opportunity. They saw that land of opportunity and worked countless days and nights to be able to afford what is now known as Silver Star Auto Express. They have been running the Shop since 1996 and have had countless days of work on vehicles and are loyal, hard-working and honest to their customers.

Michael Dhoman

Mechanic, State Inspector and Owner

Over 35 years experience in the industry. The Owner, Michael Dhoman who started out as a Mechanic back in Guyana and came to America with his experience and worked countless days and nights to be able to afford a business and operate it under the name of Silver Star Auto Express. Michael values the customers' needs and believes that htey are of the utmost importance. As a result, a high percentage of our business is from repeat customers and referrals.

Rickford Dhoman

Mechanic

Rickford Dhoman, that also goes by Rick has over 25 years specializing in Tranmissions and Overall Automotive Repair. Rickford Dhoman is the brother of Michael Dhoman and together they act as a duo under the name Silver Star Auto Express. Also born in Guyana Rickford has had experience in the Automotive Repair field and values the customer and their trust that they have with him.
